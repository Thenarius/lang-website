Grammar: 
	SENTENCE STRUCTURE A
		A-1: (SUBJECT は) [adj][noun] です。 [copula form]
		A-2: (SUBJECT は) (OBJECT を) VERB-T。 [transitive form]
		A-3: (SUBJECT は) VERB-IN。 [intransitive form]
	DICTIONARY FORM
		見る、読む
	POLITE FORM: PRESENT/FUTURE TENSE
		見ます、読みます
	MODIFYING NOUNS WITH の
	DEMONSTRATIVE PRONOUNS: この、これ SERIES
