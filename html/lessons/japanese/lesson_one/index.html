Learning Objectives

In the first module, your job is to learn how to describe the world around you.

By the end of this module, you will be able to:

Reading and Writing

  • Understand the meaning of hiragana, katakana, and kanji and tell which one to use when.
  • Read and write (by hand!) all 46 hiragana and katakana characters.
  • Read and write 35 basic kanji.
  • Read and write numbers, times, and days of the week in Japanese.

Grammar

  • Use simple sentences to describe people, places and things:　私はがくせいです。
  • Tell about what you or another person is doing right now: [noun] は [verb]。
  • Use sentences with transitive verbs and direct objects to tell more specific details about what you or someone else is doing: [noun]は[object]を[verb]。
  • Describe things using adjectives, to tell what they look like, and nouns, to tell who or what they belong to. (い・な adjectives; [noun]の[noun])
